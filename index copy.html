How to prepare copper water

With our pure copper water bottle, it is very easy to prepare copper enriched water. You don’t have to do much to prepare copper treated water. All you need is our pure copper water bottle and filtered drinking water. Every night before going to bed, fill your copper water bottle with fresh drinking water and keep it near your bedside. During the night, tiny particles of copper would leach into the water making it copper charged.

It is important to leave the water overnight or for at least 6 hours in order to get the maximum benefits. It is recommended to drink this copper charged water as the first thing in the morning when you wake up, in order to reap the maximum benefits. Copper water is best consumed at room temperature, so it is recommended not to store copper water in the refrigerator.
